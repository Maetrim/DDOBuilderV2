Automatic Feats Pane

The Automatci Feats pane display can be toggled by clicking the button in the Standard Toolbar.

Menus: File menu | Edit menu | Development menu | Gear menu | Settings menu | View menu | Help menu

Panes: Breakdowns | Builds | Class and Levels | DCs | Enhancements | Epic Destinies | Granted Feats | Inventory | Log | Notes | Past Lives | Quests and Favor | Reaper Enhancements | Skills | Spells and SLAs | Stances

The Automatic Feats pane shows the feats that are automatically assigned at the level that is selected in the Class and Levels view.

As you mov ethe mouse over the Class and Pane view the list is updated. If you move the mouse off the Class and Pane view, the last highlighted level remains in the Automatic Feats view and you can then move the mouse over each "Automatic" feat in the list to get its description.
