Past Lives and Special Feats Pane

The Past Lives and Special Feats Pane display can be toggled by clicking the button in the Standard Toolbar.

The Past Lives and Special Feats Pane is split up into section depending on the type of the past lives. These are:

  • Heroic - All heroic "Class Completionist" feats. Listed alphabetically left to right, except for Archetypes which are listed to the immediate right of its base class.
  • Racial - One entry for each race for racial past lives, listed alphabetically left to right
  • Iconic - One for each Iconic class, listed alphabetically left to right.
  • Epic - Once for each Epic past life, list alphabetically left to right by Sphere and Epic Past life name.
  • Inherent - One for each special "Tome" that is available in game. Inherent Tomes may have different max stacking values.
  • Favor - One for each Patron favor that can provide in game bonuses. Listed alphabetically, left to right.

You can quickly train or revoke a specific feat group by Left (Train) or Right (Revoke) clicking the group header control.

Mousing over a specific button will show details about it and any max ranks that may apply. The number shown in the button is the number of times that options has been trained.
